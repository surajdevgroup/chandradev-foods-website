Who We Are

About Chandradev Foods Private Limited

Chandradev Foods Private Limited is a leading manufacturer of premium wheat products, committed to delivering purity, freshness and consistent quality. Established in 2012 and manufacturing since 2020, we combine traditional stone grinding with modern automated technology to produce high-quality wheat flour for families and businesses.

Our Commitment to Quality

Every batch of wheat is carefully selected, cleaned, laboratory tested, stone ground and hygienically packed in our fully automated facility. Our manufacturing process ensures that every pack maintains freshness, nutrition and authentic taste.
