ABOUT US

About Chandradev Foods Private Limited

Chandradev Foods Private Limited was established in 2012 with a vision to manufacture premium wheat flour products built on quality, purity, trust, and consistency. Commercial production began in 2020, and today the company operates a modern fully automated manufacturing facility equipped with advanced milling technology, traditional stone grinding, and stringent quality control systems.

Under our flagship brand Tulsi Chakki Fresh Atta, we manufacture stone-ground wheat flour using carefully selected wheat, hygienic processing, and a fully automated production line with minimal human contact to ensure freshness, nutrition, and consistent quality in every pack.
