Our Manufacturing Process

Every stage of our manufacturing process is carefully monitored to ensure premium quality, hygiene, and consistency from wheat procurement to final dispatch.

01

Premium Wheat Procurement

High-quality wheat is carefully sourced from trusted suppliers to ensure superior raw material.

02

3-Stage Cleaning

Wheat passes through multiple cleaning stages to remove dust, stones, impurities, and unwanted particles.

03

Traditional Stone Grinding

Carefully milled using traditional stone grinding to preserve natural flavour, aroma, and nutrition.

04

Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes laboratory testing for moisture, gluten, granulation, and other quality parameters.

05

Automatic Hygienic Packing

Products are packed using fully automated systems to ensure hygiene, freshness, and product safety.

06

Dispatch & Distribution

Finished products are stored and dispatched through our reliable distribution network across multiple states.
